Housing Market in the 10 Best Cities in US with Tech Jobs

Project Objectives

This tool was designed with software professionals in mind. If you’re considering relocating for a technology-related job, chances are you’ll be shopping around one of these 10 cities, which were selected because they showed the highest volume of software job postings per Glassdoor as of April 2018.

Explore the information below, then use the sortable list to your left to rank these 10 cities. The images included at the top of this page represent each city. Hover over each image for the corresponding city name.
