Housing Market In The Top 10 Tech Cities

Project Objectives

This tool was designed with software professionals in mind. If you’re considering relocating within the U.S. for a technology-related job, chances are you’ll be shopping around one of these 10 cities, which were selected because they showed the highest volume of software job postings per Glassdoor as of April 2018.

Explore the information below, then use the sortable list to your left to rank these 10 cities. The images included at the top of this page represent each city.

Average Number of Sales

This chart will display a year-by-year comparison for average number of sales in the selected city from 2008 through 2018.

As the tech industry has expanded, housing markets have also shown increased activity within these metro areas.

Average Housing Price

This interactive map provides an at-a-glance view of the housing prices across all 10 cities. Use the checkboxes in the top right corner to update the map view with circle markers on each city.

The circle size reflects average housing prices. The larger the circle, the higher the price.
